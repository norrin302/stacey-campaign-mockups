Why City Council?

A Personal Commitment

Fairfax isn't just where I live—it's where my family has put down roots. When I walk through our neighborhoods, I see the potential for even safer streets, better schools, and a local government that truly listens. That's why I'm running for City Council.

This community has given so much to my family, and I believe it's my responsibility to give back. I'm not a politician—I'm a neighbor who wants to make sure every voice is heard in the decisions that shape our city's future.

What Drives Me

I believe in practical, common-sense solutions. When infrastructure projects drag on, I ask why. When schools face budget challenges, I dig into the numbers. When neighbors have concerns about safety, I listen—really listen—and work toward real answers.

My Approach

Too often, local government feels distant from the people it serves. I want to change that. My approach is simple: show up, listen carefully, ask good questions, and follow through. No agendas hidden behind jargon—just honest work on behalf of our community.

🎯 Practical Solutions

Focus on what works, not what makes headlines. Real progress happens in the details.

👂 Active Listening

Every resident has valuable insight. The best ideas often come from unexpected places.

🤝 Collaboration

City Council works best when we work together. Partisanship has no place in potholes.

📊 Data-Informed

Good decisions need good information. I dig into the facts before taking a position.

For Our Future

Fairfax is growing, and that brings both opportunities and challenges. I want to make sure that growth serves everyone—longtime residents and newcomers alike. That means smart development, strong schools, and safe neighborhoods where families can thrive for generations.

I'm asking for your vote because I believe we can do better—together. Let's keep Fairfax a place we're proud to call home.
