Where Stacy Stands

Practical solutions for Fairfax—listening first, acting responsibly.

🛣️

Infrastructure & Transportation

Stacy has championed road repairs and pedestrian safety. In the past 4 years, she helped secure funding for 12 miles of road improvements and 5 new crosswalks near schools.

🛡️

Public Safety

Supporting first responders with modern equipment and training. Stacy advocates for community policing and neighborhood watch programs that keep Fairfax safe.

🎓

Education & Schools

Strong schools mean strong neighborhoods. Stacy partners with FCPS to ensure resources for teachers, safe facilities, and programs that prepare students for success.

💼

Economic Development

Small businesses are the heart of Fairfax. Stacy supports policies that help local entrepreneurs thrive while attracting new investment to our city.

🌳

Environment & Parks

Protecting green spaces and improving parks. Stacy supports sustainable practices that keep Fairfax beautiful for future generations.

🤝

Community Engagement

Transparent, responsive government. Stacy holds regular town halls and ensures residents have a voice in decisions that affect their neighborhoods.
