The Issues

Clear priorities for a stronger Fairfax

🛣️

Infrastructure & Transportation

Fairfax deserves roads that work, traffic that flows, and infrastructure built for the long term.

  • Repair and maintain our streets systematically
  • Improve traffic flow at key intersections
  • Invest in pedestrian and bike safety
  • Support public transit options for all residents
🛡️

Public Safety

Safe neighborhoods are the foundation of a thriving community.

  • Fund and support police, fire, and EMS services
  • Expand community policing initiatives
  • Invest in mental health crisis response
  • Strengthen neighborhood watch programs
🎓

Education & Youth

Strong schools build strong communities.

  • Support school infrastructure and resources
  • Expand after-school and enrichment programs
  • Strengthen school-city partnerships
  • Invest in youth recreation activities
