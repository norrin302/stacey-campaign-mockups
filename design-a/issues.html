Where Stacy Stands

Practical solutions for Fairfax—listening first, acting responsibly.

🛣️ Infrastructure & Transportation

Stacy championed road repairs and pedestrian safety. Helped secure funding for 12 miles of road improvements and 5 new crosswalks near schools.

🛡️ Public Safety

Supporting first responders with modern equipment and training. Advocates for community policing and neighborhood watch programs.

🎓 Education & Schools

Strong schools mean strong neighborhoods. Partners with FCPS to ensure resources for teachers and student success.

💼 Economic Development

Small businesses are the heart of Fairfax. Supports policies that help local entrepreneurs thrive.

🌳 Environment & Parks

Protecting green spaces and improving parks. Supports sustainable practices for future generations.

🤝 Community Engagement

Transparent, responsive government. Holds regular town halls and ensures residents have a voice.
