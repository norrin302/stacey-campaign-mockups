Background & Experience

Stacy R. Hall

Controller | City Council Member | Community Advocate

Stacy Hall brings a unique combination of financial expertise, local government experience, and deep community roots to her work on Fairfax City Council. As a certified public accountant and controller at a veterinary research clinic, she understands the importance of fiscal responsibility and smart budgeting.

Elected to City Council in 2022, Stacy has focused on infrastructure improvements, public safety, and ensuring transparency in local government. She serves on several committees and is known for her thorough preparation and willingness to ask tough questions.
