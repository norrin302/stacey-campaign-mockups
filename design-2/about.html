About Stacy

Stacy R. Hall brings a warm, community‑first approach to public service. As a longtime Fairfax resident and Controller at Vet Research Clinic, she blends fiscal responsibility with deep local ties.

Her career spans healthcare finance, city council leadership, and grassroots activism. Stacy believes in listening first, then acting decisively to improve neighborhoods, schools, and safety.

“Fairfax deserves steady, compassionate leadership,” she says. “I’m here to make sure every voice is heard and every dollar works harder for our families.”
