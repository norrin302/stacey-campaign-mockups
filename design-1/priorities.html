Key Priorities

Building a stronger Fairfax together

🛣️

Infrastructure

Fairfax deserves safe, well-maintained roads and infrastructure that supports our growing community. Stacy has been a leading voice for smart investments in our transportation network, ensuring that residents can get where they need to go safely and efficiently.

Key Initiatives:

  • Home
  • Accelerate road repaving and pothole repair programs
  • Improve traffic signal timing to reduce congestion
  • Expand pedestrian and bicycle infrastructure
  • Invest in long-term maintenance of water and sewer systems
  • Support smart development that complements existing neighborhoods
🛡️

Public Safety

Safe neighborhoods are the foundation of a thriving community. Stacy works closely with first responders, community organizations, and residents to ensure Fairfax remains a safe place for families to live, work, and play.

Key Initiatives:

  • Home
  • Ensure police and fire departments have resources they need
  • Support community policing programs that build trust
  • Expand neighborhood watch and safety programs
  • Improve emergency response coordination
  • Address traffic safety in residential areas
🎓

Education

Strong schools make strong communities. Stacy believes in supporting our educators, ensuring students have the resources they need, and maintaining the partnership between the city and our school system.

Key Initiatives:

  • Home
  • Advocate for adequate school funding at all levels
  • Support after-school and enrichment programs
  • Ensure safe routes to school for students walking and biking
  • Promote workforce development and vocational training
  • Strengthen ties between schools and community organizations
