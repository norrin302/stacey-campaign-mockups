About Stacy

A lifetime of service to Fairfax

Stacy R. Hall has dedicated her career to serving the Fairfax community. As a longtime resident and public servant, she understands the challenges facing our neighborhoods and has a proven track record of delivering results.

Currently serving as Controller at Vet Research Clinic, Stacy brings financial expertise and careful stewardship to every role she takes on. Her experience managing complex budgets and ensuring accountability makes her uniquely qualified to oversee the city's fiscal responsibilities.

Stacy's commitment to public service extends beyond her professional career. She has been an active volunteer in local schools, community organizations, and civic groups, always working to make Fairfax a better place for families and businesses alike.

"I believe in listening first," Stacy says. "When residents take the time to share their concerns, they deserve a council member who not only hears them but acts on their behalf."
