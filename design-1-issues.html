The Issues

Clear priorities for a stronger Fairfax—infrastructure, safety, and education that works for everyone

01

Infrastructure & Transportation

Fairfax deserves roads that work, traffic that flows, and infrastructure built for the long term. Stacy supports smart investments that keep our community connected.

  • Repair and maintain our streets systematically
  • Improve traffic flow at key intersections
  • Invest in pedestrian and bike safety
  • Support public transit options that serve all residents
02

Public Safety

Safe neighborhoods are the foundation of a thriving community. Stacy believes in supporting our first responders while modernizing safety programs for today's challenges.

  • Fund and support police, fire, and EMS services
  • Expand community policing initiatives
  • Invest in mental health crisis response
  • Strengthen neighborhood watch and safety programs
03

Education & Youth

Strong schools build strong communities. Stacy partners with educators and parents to ensure every child has the opportunity to succeed.

  • Support school infrastructure and resources
  • Expand after-school and enrichment programs
  • Strengthen school-city partnerships
  • Invest in youth recreation and activities
