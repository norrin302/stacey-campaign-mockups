About Stacy

Stacy R. Hall brings a bold, results‑driven approach to Fairfax City Council. As Controller at Vet Research Clinic, she blends fiscal acumen with deep community roots.

Her track record includes modernizing public safety, championing infrastructure upgrades, and advocating for quality education.

“Fairfax deserves a steady, decisive voice that turns ideas into action,” Stacy says.
